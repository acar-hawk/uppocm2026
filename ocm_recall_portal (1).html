⚠ Risk: Aspergillus mold (55 lots)  ·  Heavy metal cadmium (1 lot)

Following inspections and a records audit conducted between December 2025 and January 2026, OCM determined that Keystone State Testing New York issued unreliable test results for several adult-use cannabis products. Products tested by this laboratory may not have met required state safety and testing standards.

This recall is precautionary. No adverse health effects have been reported. Investigation is ongoing. Dispensaries and distributors must immediately cease distribution and sales of affected products.
